FAQ

Questions you might have

🧼 Do I need to provide cleaning supplies?
No! A Mom and a Mop provides all of the professional cleaning supplies needed to leave your home sparkling. The only item we ask clients to provide is their vacuum, as this helps prevent the spread of allergens, pet hair, and bacteria from one home to another.
🐾 Do you clean homes with pets?
Absolutely! We love our furry friends and understand that pet hair is just part of life. Whether you have dogs, cats, or other pets, we're happy to clean your home with them in mind.
📅 Can I reschedule my appointment?
Of course! We understand that life happens. We simply ask for at least 48 hours' notice if you need to reschedule your cleaning appointment.
🚗 Do you travel?
Yes! We do travel outside our primary service area. Travel availability depends on the distance, so just send us a message with your location and we'll let you know if we can accommodate you.
🔑 Do I need to be home during the cleaning?
Not at all! Many of our clients are away while we clean. You are welcome to be home if you prefer, but it's not required. For your peace of mind, A Mom and a Mop is background check approved, so you can feel confident your home is in trustworthy hands.
